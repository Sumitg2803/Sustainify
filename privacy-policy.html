Privacy Policy

Sustainify respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us to help communities.

Last Updated: March 2026

How We Handle Your Information

Please read these terms carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Sustainify ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy while you use our community donation platform. Sustainify is designed to connect generous individuals and organizations with those in need by facilitating the donation of clothes, books, toys, and other essential items.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website. Maintaining your trust is our top priority, and we only collect what is strictly necessary to make the donation process seamless and effective for the communities we serve.

2. Information We Collect

When you interact with the Sustainify platform—particularly when using our donation features or contacting us—we may ask you to provide certain personal information. The types of data we may collect include:

  • Your full name (or organization name)
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Geographic location (city or pickup area)
  • Specific details about the donation items (category, condition, size, quantity)
  • Images or photographs uploaded to represent your donation

We specifically gather this information solely to facilitate the listing of your donations and to enable efficient communication between donors and prospective recipients.

3. How We Use Information

The information we collect is utilized strictly to provide, maintain, and improve our community services. We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Creating and managing public donation listings on the "Available Donations" board.
  • Connecting contributors with recipients or partner non-governmental organizations (NGOs).
  • Sending you administrative messages, such as confirmation of your submitted donations.
  • Improving the platform's functionality, UI/UX, and overall user experience.
  • Responding to your inquiries, support requests, and feedback submitted via our Contact page.
  • Featuring you on the "Wall of Kindness" to recognize your contributions (with your implicit or explicit consent).

4. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. Sustainify implements a variety of reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, use, alteration, or disclosure.

Our platform simulates local environments utilizing modern browser technologies like LocalStorage to process your data without exposing it to vulnerable external databases where unnecessary. While we prioritize the secure handling of your data, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Information is only shared under the following limited circumstances necessary to fulfill the mission of Sustainify:

  • Public Listings: Information you voluntarily include in your donation submission (such as location and item descriptions) is shared publicly to allow users in need to view and request the items.
  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our operations, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with legal obligations or protect the rights and safety of Sustainify and our users.

6. User Rights and Choices

We believe in empowering you with control over your own data. Because our platform is community-driven, you retain specific rights regarding your information. At any time, users may:

  • Request the immediate removal or modification of active donation listings.
  • Request the deletion of their personal data from our active simulated servers.
  • Update or correct inaccurate personal information regarding contact details or organization identity.
  • Opt-out of future communications or being featured on the public Wall of Kindness.

7. Cookies and Website Analytics

Sustainify may utilize basic website technologies, such as cookies, web beacons, and local browser storage, to enhance and personalize your user experience.

These technologies allow the website to remember your actions and preferences over time so you do not have to keep re-entering them whenever you come back to the site or browse from one page to another. We may also use non-identifiable analytics to understand how users interact with our platform (e.g., tracking the most visited SDGs or filtering habits) to continuously improve the interface and features.

8. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to external resources, websites, and plugins that are not operated by us (for example, linking to official United Nations SDG websites). If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site.

We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit. Sustainify has no control over and assumes no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

9. Children's Privacy

Sustainify's services are built for adults, organizations, and families. Our platform is not intended for unsupervised use by minors under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us so that we will be able to take necessary actions to remove that information from our servers.

10. Updates to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date prominently at the top of the header.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

11. Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data handling practices, we are here to help. You can contact our dedicated privacy team through the following channels:
