About Sustainify

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the team driving this initiative forward.

Who Are We?

Sustainify is a modern, smart, and responsive food donation platform. Our primary goal is to foster a culture of food sharing where surplus is redirected to where it is needed most.

We work locally to ensure that transportation is simple and immediate, creating a network of donors, volunteers, and NGOs that seamlessly interact to prevent food spoilage and support vulnerable communities.

Why It Matters

Every day, staggering amounts of freshly prepared food by restaurants, households, and event organizers are thrown away simply because there is no immediate channel to redistribute it.

This platform matters because it removes the friction from food donation. It uses technology to bridge the critical time-gap between when food becomes "surplus" and when it becomes "waste".

Key Platform Features

Real-time Expiry Tracking

Dynamic countdown timers ensure that users are immediately aware of the freshness status of available donations.

Smart Filtering

Effortlessly browse available donations based on dietary preferences such as Vegetarian or Non-Vegetarian.

Fully Responsive UI

Accessible on any device – mobile, tablet, or desktop – to ensure you can donate or claim food on the go.

Academic Project

About the Project

This platform was developed as part of an academic requirement to showcase how web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap) can be utilized to solve real-world problems. The application runs entirely on the client-side utilizing modern browser APIs to simulate a full-stack experience without a backend.
