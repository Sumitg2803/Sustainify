What is Sustainify?

We are a centralized hub designed to reduce waste and fight poverty simultaneously. Often, items that sit unused in our homes—like jackets, toys, laptops, or books—can be life-changing for someone else.

Sustainify allows individuals and NGOs to post or request items. From everyday clothes to school supplies, we make charitable giving transparent, direct, and incredibly easy.

  • Free coordination platform
  • Support for multiple item categories
  • Direct point-to-point donation

What You Can Donate

We accept a wide variety of essential items to help build stronger, happier communities. Discover the areas where you can make an impact.

Clothes
