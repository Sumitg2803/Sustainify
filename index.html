What is Sustainify?

Sustainify is an intelligent bridge between excess food and true need. Whether you're a restaurant with surplus meals after closing, or an NGO helping the community, our platform connects you seamlessly.

We leverage local connectivity to ensure fresh, usable food doesn't end up in landfills but instead reaches those who can benefit from it, promoting a circular and compassionate economy.

Why We Are Doing This

  • Massive Food Waste

    Tons of edible food are discarded daily while perfectly safe for consumption.

  • Rising Hunger

    Millions struggle to afford daily meals, creating a stark contrast to the waste.

  • Lack of Coordination

    Donors want to help, but lack a clear, fast way to reach recipients before food spoils.

How It Works

Three simple steps to make a huge difference.

Step 1: Donate Food

Post details of your surplus food, including location and expiry time.

Step 2: Find Donations

NGOs or individuals browse available fresh food in their local area.

Step 3: Collect Food

Food is secured and collected directly from the donor before it expires.
