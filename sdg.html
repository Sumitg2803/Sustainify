Our Commitment to the Future

Sustainify aligns proudly with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals to build a better, greener world.

What are the UN SDGs?

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are a universal call to action, established by the United Nations in 2015, to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure that by 2030 all people enjoy peace and prosperity.

There are 17 goals in total, all interconnected. At Sustainify, we believe technology and community collaboration can directly impact several of these vital objectives.

Goals We Support

Goal 2: Zero Hunger

By redirecting perfectly good food from landfills to the plates of those who need it, Sustainify aims to end hunger in local communities.

Goal 11: Sustainable Cities

We foster community solidarity, making urban and local systems more resilient through responsible resource sharing and coordination.

Goal 12: Responsible Consumption

We provide a practical solution to halve per capita global food waste at retail and consumer levels by rescuing surplus production.

Our Major Contributions

Reducing Food Waste

Providing an active and rapid network to consume surplus food, keeping it out of municipal landfills.

Helping Communities

Bridging the gap between food abundance and food scarcity within the same neighborhoods.

Promoting Sustainability

Raising awareness about the environmental cost of food production and the importance of conservation.

Encouraging Responsible Habits

Making it incredibly easy for businesses to adopt a "zero-waste" approach to their surplus provisions.
